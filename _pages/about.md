<h2 class="section-heading">About Me</h2>

<p>I am an Associate Professor in the School of Electronic and Information Engineering at <strong>Lanzhou Jiaotong University</strong> (兰州交通大学), and a Visiting Scholar at Queen's University Belfast, UK. I obtained my Ph.D. from Beijing Jiaotong University (北京交通大学).</p>

<p>My research interests primarily focus on <strong>time series analysis, causal learning, evolutionary computation, and uncertain artificial intelligence</strong>. I have published over 17 academic papers in top-tier journals and conferences, including IEEE TFS, KBS, ASC, Acta Automatica Sinica, CVPR, and WACV.</p>

<p>I have led and participated in several research projects, including the Fundamental Research Funds for the Central Universities, NSFC, National Key R&D Program of China, and National Key Laboratory Open Projects. I hold 5 software copyrights and 3 invention patents.</p>
